AI Assistant Acceptable Use Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our AI Assistant! This policy outlines the acceptable use of our AI-powered assistant, explaining its capabilities, limitations, and the guidelines for interacting with it. The AI assistant is designed to assist you with a wide range of tasks and questions, but it is important to recognize that it is still in active development.

By using this AI assistant, you agree to follow this policy and understand the limitations outlined here.

2. Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this AI Assistant is to provide helpful, informative, and engaging responses to user queries across various topics. However, it is important to note that the AI Assistant may not always provide accurate, complete, or satisfactory responses.

This policy applies to all interactions with the AI assistant, whether via text, voice, or other interfaces.

3. AI Assistant Limitations

3.1 Inaccurate or Incomplete Answers

Our AI Assistant uses machine learning algorithms and vast data sources to generate responses. However, there are situations where the assistant may provide incorrect, incomplete, or outdated information. Possible reasons include:

  • The AI model may not have access to the most up-to-date information.
  • Some topics may not be well-represented in the data the AI is trained on.
  • The assistant may misinterpret the user's question or request, leading to incorrect responses.

While the AI Assistant is constantly improving, it is still a work in progress. Users should verify important information independently and consult with experts where necessary.

3.2 Development Stage

The AI Assistant is continuously evolving. Our team is actively working to improve its performance, accuracy, and understanding of complex topics. As a result:

  • The AI Assistant may occasionally provide answers that are suboptimal or incorrect.
  • Some queries may lead to unexpected or nonsensical responses.
  • It is possible that the assistant will not have an answer for certain specialized or rare topics.

We appreciate your patience as we continue to develop and refine the AI system.

4. Acceptable Use

4.1 Appropriate Interactions

When using the AI Assistant, users are expected to engage in a respectful and constructive manner. Acceptable use includes:

  • Asking questions or seeking help on a wide variety of topics.
  • Engaging with the AI Assistant to obtain information, suggestions, or advice.
  • Providing feedback on incorrect or incomplete answers to help improve the system.

4.2 Prohibited Use

The AI Assistant is not intended to be used for malicious, harmful, or inappropriate purposes. Prohibited uses include:

  • Using the assistant to generate offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
  • Attempting to manipulate the AI into providing harmful or dangerous advice.
  • Using the assistant to deceive or mislead others.
  • Violating any laws or regulations while interacting with the AI.
  • Using the AI to be dishonest or cheat on school or work projects.

5. Transparency and Error Acknowledgment

5.1 Wrong or Incomplete Answers

If the AI Assistant provides an incorrect or incomplete answer, please keep the following in mind:

  • The AI Assistant may not always understand complex or nuanced questions perfectly, and some responses may not be entirely accurate.
  • While the assistant strives for accuracy, it is not infallible and may occasionally produce answers that need further clarification or correction.
  • If you notice that the assistant has given an incorrect response, you can rephrase your question, try different keywords, or consult other sources for verification.

5.2 AI Assistant Development Feedback

We encourage users to provide feedback when they encounter issues, inaccuracies, or limitations. This feedback will help us improve the AI system and make it more reliable. By submitting feedback, you contribute to the development process and the overall enhancement of the AI Assistant.

6. User Responsibilities

6.1 Independent Verification

The AI Assistant is a tool designed to assist and complement your work, not to replace expert advice or independent research. Users are encouraged to:

  • Verify critical information obtained from the AI Assistant with trusted sources.
  • Use the AI Assistant as a starting point for research, not as a sole resource, especially for high-stakes or important decisions.

6.2 Reporting Issues

If you encounter issues with the AI Assistant, including incorrect answers or any behavior that violates this policy, please report it to us immediately. Your input is essential in helping us improve the system.

7. Acknowledgment of Limitations

By interacting with the AI Assistant, users acknowledge and understand the following:

  • The AI Assistant is still in development and may not always provide fully accurate or reliable answers.
  • The assistant is a tool meant to assist, not replace human judgment or expert advice.
  • While we strive for continuous improvement, the AI Assistant may continue to have limitations, including occasional errors, misunderstandings, and incomplete responses.

8. Updates to the Policy

As the AI Assistant evolves, this policy may be updated to reflect new features, improvements, and changes to its use. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about its guidelines and best practices.
