HTML Text

HTML Text is a way to show text on the page.
There are many different types of text and here are the elements: h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6, and p.
H1 is heading 1 with it being the biggest and H6 being the smallest heading.
Also p stands for paragraph.

 <h2<Heading</h2>
